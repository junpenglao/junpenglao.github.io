New site. Yes, I chose the typeface; yes, there’s a tasteful off-white, and
almost a watermark. I’ve been Patrick Bateman about the kerning so you don’t
have to be.

I’ll be writing here more often, too. Working with an LLM bumped my step size
past the smooth curvature — I fly off into a divergent transition, and the draft
actually ships.

Be glad it’s a blog and not a podcast.
